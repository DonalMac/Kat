Projects

  • Approach

    Our first goal is to establish emotional composure and mental clarity toward your situation. It’s hard to think clearly under strain. When emotion takes the lead, focus narrows and clarity fades. Without enough internal bandwidth, even everyday situations can start to feel overwhelming or unclear. That’s where counselling helps. You might think of it as borrowing a brain – seeking perspective, insight, and calm from someone less entangled in the moment. When we’re overwhelmed or stuck, it’s often because something important isn’t seen or fully understood. My role is to help bring those patterns into focus and to provide you with the support and skills you need to move forward

    Therapy

    We all have a range of emotional and physical needs. When these are met in balance, we tend to feel steady and well. But when key needs are neglected or blocked, distress often follows. This principle applies to everyone; yet, how it unfolds in your life is unique. That’s why my approach is always shaped around your personal story and circumstances. I blend a broad set of evidence-based methods, including visualisation and other practical techniques. The aim is simple: to help you gain insight, restore clarity, and create meaningful change in your everyday life..

  • Featured Project

    Results

    Therapy doesn’t need to take years. Most people don’t want long-term therapy — they want a life that works. That’s why I avoid fixed expectations around how long our work will take. It depends entirely on your situation and what meaningful progress looks and feels like for you. Still, it helps to have a sense of what to expect. In most cases, I meet with clients four to six times — sometimes less. For many people, time is limited. Unless we agree otherwise, our work will focus on making the clearest, most useful progress in the shortest time possible. So, how will you know when that progress has been made? You’ll feel properly heard and understood. You’ll have a clearer grasp of what’s happening — and the tools to move forward with greater confidence and control. And if more time is needed, we’ll recognise that together.
